2026-07-19 08:55 — Founder video: pan/zoom jerk-back race

Second recording showed the board snapping to an earlier camera after fast pan/zoom. Root cause: live viewport updates persisted preference, parent setCanvasPreference echoed the response, and useEffect([preference]) re-applied that (often stale) camera over the live ref. A second contributor: the drill-camera effect re-ran on surface/board changes and could animate back to a fit target.

Fix: hydrate preference once per project; never re-apply persist echoes; debounce persist always reads viewportRef; ignore out-of-order preference saves; camera animation only on drill-scope change (not resize/board refresh); selection persist uses live camera ref.
